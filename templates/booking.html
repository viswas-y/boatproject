About this experience

Enjoy complete privacy on your own deluxe private houseboat in the Alleppey backwaters. An onboard chef prepares authentic Kerala meals just for you, served fresh as the boat drifts through palm-lined canals and the open waters of Vembanad Lake. Wake up to mist over the water and a quiet breakfast on deck.

☀ Private AC bedroom 🍽 4 meals included ⚓ Exclusive houseboat 👤 3-member crew 🌍 Village canal route

A day on the water

12:00 PM
Check-in & welcome drink
Board your private houseboat at the jetty and settle in with a chilled tender coconut welcome drink.
12:30 PM
Cruise begins on Vembanad Lake
Drift past paddy fields, fishing villages, and coir-making hamlets along the open lake.
1:30 PM
Traditional Kerala lunch (sadya)
A multi-course vegetarian feast on a banana leaf, prepared fresh by your onboard chef.

Narrow village canal route
Slow cruise through quieter canals lined with coconut palms and waterside homes.
6:30 PM
Sunset on deck
Anchor for the evening and watch the sky turn gold over the backwaters with snacks & tea.
8:00 PM
Dinner & overnight stay
A home-style Kerala dinner, followed by a peaceful night anchored on the water.
9:00 AM
Breakfast & check-out
A leisurely breakfast on deck before returning to the jetty for check-out.
