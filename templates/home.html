Our story

Rooted in the rhythms of Kerala

Backwater Escape was born from a love of this landscape — its still canals, swaying palms, and villages where time slows down. We are not a resort chain. We are a small, family-run team from Alappuzha who believe travel should leave you changed.

Every experience we offer — from a private houseboat overnight to a dawn kayak through narrow village channels — is built around authentic connection: with the water, with Kerala's culture, and with yourself.
