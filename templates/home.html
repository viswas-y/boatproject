About LagoonNest

Born on the Backwaters, Built for Wanderers

LagoonNest began when a family of Kerala fishermen converted their ancestral kettuvallam into the backwaters' most-loved floating retreat. Fifteen years on, we run a fleet of twelve fully-equipped, solar-assisted houseboats navigating Vembanad Lake and the Kuttanad canals.


Every stay is guided by people who grew up on these waters — local crew, local cooks, local knowledge. We keep our footprint light and our memories lasting.

  • Certified eco-friendly solar-powered vessels
  • All-inclusive Kerala meals by on-board chef
  • Air-conditioned private cabins with en-suite bathrooms
  • Personal naturalist guide on every overnight cruise
  • Free kayak pickup from Alleppey boat jetty
  • 24/7 captain & crew support throughout your stay
